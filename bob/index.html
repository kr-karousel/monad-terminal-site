🟣 What is BOB Terminal?
A real-time trading terminal for the BOB token on Monad — built by the community, for the community. Watch live trades, share your thoughts, and vibe with fellow BOBbers as the market moves.
🔗 Wallet Connection
Connecting your wallet is optional and is used solely for:
· Setting your nickname · Live chat · Shout on chart · Buying & selling BOB

We do not collect, store, or share your wallet data.
💜 Revenue & BOB Fees
BOB Terminal generates revenue through two features:
· 📢 Shout — BOB pinned on the chart
· ✏️ Nickname — BOB to set your permanent nickname

Revenue is split 50% to Dev and 50% returned to the community based on contribution points.
Settlement on the last day of each month.
· 💰 Revenue Dashboard — check your points & estimated payout in the top nav

📐 Point System
• Chat Activity: 1 point per active hour (max 1pt/hr, not per message)
• Nickname Change: 10 points per change

💡 Payout Formula
Dev Balance × 50% × (Your Points ÷ Total Community Points)
