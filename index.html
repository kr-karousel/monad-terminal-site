About

Monad Terminal is an independent, community-driven project built to make on-chain data accessible and visual for Monad ecosystem traders. Each terminal provides live price charts, real-time buy/sell feeds, holder rankings, whale alerts, and in-app chat — all sourced directly from the blockchain. No backend servers, no data intermediaries.

Revenue Structure
💰 Community Revenue Share
🚫 This platform will never launch a token under any circumstances.
Revenue is distributed monthly based on contribution.
Terminal revenue comes from two paid features — Shout (pin a message on the chart) and Nickname (set a permanent username).

50% → Dev wallet  ·  50% → Community (based on contribution points)

How points are earned:
· Chat activity — 1 point per active hour (max 1/hr)
· Nickname change — 10 points per change
· Shout — 10 points each

Payout formula: Dev Balance × 50% × (Your Points ÷ Total Community Points)

Settlement on the last day of each month. Check your contribution in the 💰 Revenue Dashboard inside each terminal.
